üîç Source - Finding the Right Data

Identify and connect to all relevant data sources across the health system:

  • Health Information Systems: DHIS2, OpenMRS, other EMR systems
  • Financial Systems: Insurance claims, budget systems, revenue tracking
  • Supply Chain Systems: eLMIS, pharmacy systems, inventory management
  • Laboratory Systems: LIMS, diagnostic equipment interfaces
  • Community Data: Mobile health apps, CHW reports, surveys
  • External Sources: Weather data, population census, geographic data

üì• Ingest - Building the Real-World Model

Transform diverse data into a unified model that accurately represents your health system:

  • Data Integration: Connect data from multiple sources into coherent entities
  • Domain Modeling: Structure data into our 6 key domains (Facility, People, Finance, etc.)
  • Standardization: Apply FHIR standards and common vocabularies
  • Quality Assurance: Validate, clean, and ensure data consistency
  • Real-time Processing: Stream data for immediate availability
  • Historical Context: Maintain trends and longitudinal patient records

‚ú® Enrich - Adding Intelligence

Apply advanced analytics and AI to transform raw data into actionable intelligence:

  • Predictive Models: Forecast disease outbreaks, predict high-risk pregnancies
  • Risk Algorithms: Calculate patient risk scores, identify vulnerable populations
  • Pattern Detection: Discover trends, anomalies, and hidden insights
  • Optimization Engines: Recommend optimal resource allocation and interventions
  • Benchmarking: Compare performance against standards and best practices
  • Machine Learning: Continuously improve predictions based on outcomes

üìä Serve - Delightful Data Delivery

Present insights through intuitive, beautiful interfaces that users love:

  • Interactive Dashboards: Real-time visualizations for different user roles
  • Mobile Apps: Access insights on-the-go for field workers
  • Control Rooms: Large-screen displays for emergency coordination
  • Automated Reports: Scheduled insights delivered to email/WhatsApp
  • APIs: Enable third-party systems to access processed data
  • Natural Language: AI-powered chat interfaces for querying data

üéØ Engage - Driving the Right Decisions

Ensure insights lead to actions that improve health outcomes:

  • Decision Support: Provide clear recommendations with evidence
  • Alert Systems: Proactive notifications for critical situations
  • Workflow Integration: Embed insights into daily operations
  • Performance Tracking: Monitor if decisions lead to improvements
  • Feedback Loops: Learn from outcomes to refine recommendations
  • Change Management: Support users in adopting data-driven practices
